Steering Committee

The steering committee is a group prominent scientists and leaders that were selected to represent key segments and application areas of critical importance to the UPittISC consortium objectives. The advisory group provides the faculty leadership team with insights about emerging needs and trends within relevant industries and across the various agencies.
